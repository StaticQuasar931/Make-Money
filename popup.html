Prestige

Prestiging gives you a permanent income boost. When you prestige, all your progress will be reset including upgrades and bonus clicks. However, you will get (your number of items - 10) prestige points. Each prestige point gives a +50% income boost and does not get reset when you prestige again! Prestiging is very useful if you are stuck and cannot make any progress. This way, you can prestige and gain income boost and get back there faster and make more progress.

Basics

Make Money is a simple clicking game in which you try to earn as much money as possible. Click the earn button and gain an amount of money money equal to your income.
Navigation

Make Money is split into seven main areas. The money tab is the main tab in which you can earn money. The income tab allows you to view all of your possessions. The upgrades tab contains upgrades to improve your income and items. The updates tab contains all past update notes. The help tab is for help. The settings area allows you to adjust the options of the game. The prestige area is for prestiging to gain prestige points. The casino tab is for gambling money (more information in casino section).



Items

Items increase your income, but will cost money to purchase. An item will be purchasable when you have three times the cost of the item. Items can greatly increase your income and will help you progress much faster.
Upgrades

Upgrades increase your income and make it easier to progress in the game. Upgrades cost a certain amount of money, but are usually good to purchase.
Casino

You can gamble money in the casino. You are allowed to bet any amount of money that you have. The slot machine generates 3 random numbers from 1 to 7 inclusive. Getting all 3 7s multiplies your bet by 25, while getting 2 7s multiplies your bet by 8. Getting 3 of any other number also multiplies your bet by 8, and getting 2 of any other number multiplies your bet by 3. Any other combination is a loss, and you receive no money and lose your bet.
